# CS-330
SNHU CS-330 portfolio submission

**How do I approach designing software?**
I approach software design by focusing on structure and planning before implementation. For this project in particular, I broke the scene into smaller components, making the overall design easier to manage and adjust as I worked.

**What new design skills has your work on the project helped you to craft?**
This project helped me improve my visual design skills. I learned how lighting and textures work together to create a cohesive scene.

**What design process did you follow for your project work?**
I followed an iterative process by starting with a basic scene and gradually refining it. I tested the changes and adjusted elements, such as object positioning, as needed.

**How could tactics from your design approach be applied in future work?**
Breaking problems into smaller parts and applying components one at a time can be applied to any project. This approach makes development more manageable and helps catch issues early.

**How do I approach developing programs?**
I develop programs in a step-by-step approach, starting with basic functionality and adding features over time. This helps keep the code manageable and catches major oversights early.

**What new development strategies did you use while working on your 3D scene?**
I used modular development by separating logic into functions for materials, objects, and scene setup. This improved organization and made the code easier to maintain and understand.

**How did iteration factor into your development?**
Iteration was important throughout the project. I made small changes, tested them, and refined the scene multiple times to improve the final result.

**How has your approach to developing code evolved throughout the milestones?**
My approach shifted from just getting things to work and render to the scene to focusing more on fine-tuning placement and light interactions. I became more intentional with structure as the project progressed.

**How can computer science help me in reaching my goals?**
Computer science helps me build problem-solving skills and technical skills that can be applied across different professional and personal areas. It prepares me to work with complex systems and technologies.

**How do computational graphics and visualizations give you new knowledge and skills for your educational pathway?**
This project helped me understand how mathematical concepts like transformations and lighting are applied in programming. This has introduced me to new ways of thinking about how systems are designed and how they are interacted with. 

**How do computational graphics and visualizations give you new knowledge and skills for your professional pathway?**
These skills can be applied to areas like game development, data visualization, and UI design. They also improve my ability to create functional and visually effective applications.
